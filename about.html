About Morija Arts & Cultural Festival

History

The Morija Arts & Cultural Festival is one of the most important cultural events in Lesotho. It celebrates Basotho traditions, music, art, food and heritage. The festival attracts visitors from different parts of Lesotho and international tourists.

Significance

The festival promotes Basotho culture and helps preserve traditional music, dance and crafts. It also supports local businesses and tourism in the area.

Target Tourists

  • Local tourists
  • International cultural tourists
  • Students and researchers
  • Families and festival lovers
